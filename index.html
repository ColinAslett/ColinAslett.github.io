Rating System Performance

This section will show how well each rating system predicts the winner of a college football game. The note section is my own opinion on how each rating system is performing, highlighting their weaknesses and strengths. The technical section shows how each rating system is performing from an analytical stanpoint including: brier score, log loss, and lots of graphs. This section is currently under construction.

Notes For 2020-2021 Season: With Covid-19 forcing many conferences to only play against teams within the conference the graph-based rating systems suffered. For example, Alabama has a perfect score in the SEC but is ranked lower than other teams that simply played teams outside of their conference. Another challenge for the rating systems this season was that many teams did not play the same number of games, which is crucial when the regular season is only about a dozen games to begin with.
