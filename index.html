Rating Systems Explained

  • ELO: This rating system is well known in the chess world but can be used for any game where there is a winner and loser(draws are allowed). For all major league sports the teams will start a new season at 1500, but each sport has a different K-Value which represents how much change in your rating can occur after a game. A high K-Value means a lot of change can occur and a low K-Value means little change after one game. For major league sports with a low volume of games(NFL) the K-Value is close to 100, while the K-Value for high volume major league sports is closer to 20.
